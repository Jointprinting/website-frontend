Questions

The stuff people ask first.

How fast can my site be live?

Usually 3–5 business days from the moment I have your info and a few photos. Bigger multi-page builds take a little longer, but you'll see a live draft within the first week either way.

Do I actually own the site?

Yes. Your domain, your content, your site. If you ever cancel the plan, the site is yours to keep or move — you're never locked in.

What if I need changes later?

Just text or email me. Edits are handled on your monthly plan — swap a photo, update your hours, add a service — no per-change invoices and no waiting on a ticket queue.

Will people actually find it on Google?

On Silver and up I set up your Google Business Profile and the on-page basics so you show up when locals search your trade + town. It's built to get found, not just to look good.

Do I have to write all the words?

No. Tell me about your business on a quick call and I'll draft the copy for you — you just read it over and tell me what to tweak.

What if I don't have a domain or business email yet?

I'll help you grab the domain and get everything pointed correctly, and I can set up a matching name@yourbusiness.com email too. You don't need to figure out any of the technical side.

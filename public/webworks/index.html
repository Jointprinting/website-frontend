How it works

You see it before you buy it.

No proposals, no mockups, no deposit. We build the real thing first — that’s the whole pitch.

1

Tell us about your business

Five minutes — a quick call or a short form. What you do, your hours, how customers should reach you. Photos help, but we can start without them.

2

We build your site Free — before you pay anything

A few days later you get a link to your actual website. Your name, your services, working on your phone. Not a sketch — the real thing, ready to judge.

Questions

The stuff people ask first.

What does the free build actually cost me?

Nothing. We build your site and send you a link to the real thing. If you want it live, you pick a plan and we launch it on your domain — the monthly price starts then. If you don’t, we part friends and you owe nothing.

How fast will I see my site?

Usually three to five business days from the moment we have your info. Bigger multi-page builds take a little longer, but you’ll have something real to look at within the first week either way.

Do I actually own the site?

Your domain and your content always leave with you. The site itself — the design, hosting, and every update — runs as part of your plan.

What if I need changes later?

Text or email me. Edits are part of your monthly plan — swap a photo, update your hours, add a service — no per-change bills, no ticket queue.

Will people actually find it on Google?

On Silver and up we set up your Google Business Profile and the on-page basics, so you show up when locals search your trade plus your town. The site is built to get found, not just to look good.

Do I have to write the words or have good photos?

No. Tell me about your business on a quick call and I’ll draft everything — you just read it over and tell me what to fix. Phone photos are plenty to start; the site is designed to look sharp either way.

I already have a website. Is that a problem?

It’s usually easier. We build the new one fresh, you compare it to what you have, and if it wins we move your domain over with no downtime. Your phone number, email, and Google reviews all stay put.

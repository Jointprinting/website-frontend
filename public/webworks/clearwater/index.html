Good to know

Questions before you dive in.

What's actually included in weekly service?

Every visit we test and balance the water, skim, and empty the baskets. On Full Service and up we also brush, vacuum, net, and check the filter — and every plan gets a photo report texted after each stop.

Do I have to buy the chemicals?

No — chemicals are included in the monthly price on every plan. No trips to the pool store, no guessing at chlorine, no surprise add-ons.

My pool turned green — can you save it?

Almost always, and usually within a few days. Text a photo and we'll tell you exactly what it'll take and what it'll cost before we touch it.

Do I need to be home for service?

No. As long as we can reach the gate and the equipment, you never have to be there — the photo report shows you it's done.

Am I locked into a contract?

No. Weekly service is month to month through the season — pause or cancel anytime. We keep customers because the water's clear, not because of fine print.

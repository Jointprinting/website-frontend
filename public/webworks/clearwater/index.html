Good to know

Questions before you dive in.

What's actually included in weekly service?

Every visit we test and balance the water, skim the surface, empty the baskets, and check the equipment. On Full Service and up we also brush, vacuum, and net — and you get a photo summary after each visit.

Do I have to buy the chemicals?

Nope — chemicals are included in your monthly price. No trips to the pool store, no guessing at chlorine levels, no surprise charges.

My pool turned green — can you save it?

Almost always. Our green-to-clean recovery usually has a pool swimmable again within a few days. Send a photo and we'll tell you what it'll take.

Do I need to be home for service?

No. As long as we can reach the gate and equipment, you never have to be there. You'll get a text with photos every time we visit, so you always know it's done.

Am I locked into a contract?

No lock-in — weekly service is month-to-month through the season, and you can pause or cancel anytime. We keep you because the water's clear, not because of fine print.
